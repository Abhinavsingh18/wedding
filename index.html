Haldi Ceremony Details

The Haldi ceremony is a pre-wedding ritual where a paste made from turmeric, oil, and water is applied to the bride and groom. This ceremony is known to bring good luck and bless the couple with glowing skin.

Mehndi Ceremony Details

The Mehndi ceremony is a festive event where intricate henna designs are applied to the bride's hands and feet. It's filled with music, dance, and celebrations with close family and friends.

Barat Celebration Details

The Barat is the groom's wedding procession. It's a grand celebration with music, dancing, and festivities as the groom travels to the wedding venue, usually on a horse, accompanied by his family and friends.
